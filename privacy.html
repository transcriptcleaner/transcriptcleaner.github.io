Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 2025

Welcome to Transcript Cleaner. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data in compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India).

1. No Data Storage

This tool is fully browser-based. Your text is processed locally in your device using open-source libraries (compromise, remove-punctuation, capitalize-titles) delivered via CDN. We do not upload, log, or store any transcript data on our servers.

2. Personal Data

We do not collect personal information such as names, emails, or identifiers. Any data you enter remains on your device until you close or refresh the page.

3. Cookies & Ads

This site may display third-party advertisements provided by networks such as Adsterra. These networks may use cookies to deliver more relevant ads. You can control cookies via your browser settings. By using this site, you consent to limited cookie use by such networks.

4. Legal Basis

Under the DPDP Act, we act as a “data fiduciary” only for minimal browser interactions. Since no personal data is stored or processed beyond your device, consent is deemed limited to local processing. You may revoke consent anytime by leaving the site.

5. Children’s Privacy

This tool is intended for general audiences and not targeted at children under 13.

6. External Links

Our pages may contain links to other websites. We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices.

7. Contact & Grievance Redressal

If you believe any data or content violates your rights, please email us at: grievance.transcriptcleaner@gmail.com (This contact will handle grievances, takedown notices, or DPDP-related queries.)

8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy occasionally. The latest version will always appear on this page.
